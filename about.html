Our Mission

At DataThrive, we believe health data should work for everyone—not just big institutions. That’s why we empower everyday people to take part in health research by sharing insights from the devices they already wear, like fitness trackers and smartwatches.

Inspired by our founder Elijah Grant’s personal experience, our mission is to turn passive data into active progress. We make it simple and secure for users to participate in cutting-edge studies—right from the comfort of their homes. Whether you're walking, sleeping, or tracking your heart rate, you’re not just collecting numbers—you’re fueling innovation.

And because we believe your contributions matter, we reward you for them. With DataThrive, you're not just getting points—you're driving discoveries, accelerating treatments, and making healthcare smarter for everyone.

Jordan Vance – CEO & Founder

After losing a loved one to a preventable illness, Jordan was driven to make health research more accessible and proactive. He founded DataThrive to help everyday people become active participants in shaping the future of healthcare—by simply living their lives and sharing their wearable data.

Tyler Chen – Product Manager

Tyler brings a user-first mindset to every feature. He makes sure that DataThrive’s platform is intuitive, motivating, and continuously evolving to serve our users’ needs and feedback.

Ayesha Malik – Data Scientist

Ayesha analyzes patterns from thousands of wearable data points and study results to unlock powerful insights that help researchers design smarter, more inclusive studies.

Leo Garcia – Legal & Compliance Officer

Leo ensures that DataThrive stays fully compliant with data privacy laws like HIPAA and GDPR. His mission is to make sure user trust is protected at every turn.

Nina Rojas – UX/UI Designer

Nina crafts sleek, human-centered designs that make the DataThrive experience seamless—from signing up to earning rewards. Her design work turns complex data into joyful, motivating journeys.
