Our Mission

At DataThrive, we believe health data should work for everyone—not just big institutions. That’s why we empower everyday people to take part in health research by sharing insights from the devices they already wear, like fitness trackers and smartwatches.

Our founder, Elijah Grant, was deeply affected by losing a loved one to a preventable illness. This personal loss inspired him to create DataThrive with a vision to give people ownership of their health data and a real voice in shaping the future of healthcare.

We turn passive data into active progress, making it simple and secure for users to participate in cutting-edge studies—right from the comfort of their homes. Whether you're walking, sleeping, or tracking your heart rate, you’re not just collecting numbers—you’re fueling innovation.

Because we believe your contributions matter, we reward you for them. You're not just earning points—you’re accelerating treatments and helping to make healthcare smarter and more inclusive.
