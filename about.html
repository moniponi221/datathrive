Our Mission

At DataThrive, we believe health data should work for everyone—not just big institutions. That’s why we empower everyday people to take part in health research by sharing insights from the devices they already wear, like fitness trackers and smartwatches.

Inspired by our founder Elijah Grant’s personal experience, our mission is to turn passive data into active progress. We make it simple and secure for users to participate in cutting-edge studies—right from the comfort of their homes.

Whether you're walking, sleeping, or tracking your heart rate, you’re not just collecting numbers—you’re fueling innovation. And because we believe your contributions matter, we reward you for them. You're not just getting points—you're accelerating treatments and making healthcare smarter.

Meet Our Founder

Elijah Grant – CEO & Founder

After losing a loved one to a preventable illness, Elijah Grant was driven to change how health data is used. He saw how many lives could be impacted if ordinary people had a say in research. He founded DataThrive to bridge that gap—giving people ownership of their data and a voice in shaping the future of healthcare.
